Welcome

Thank you for sharing your time and attention to be here.

I have been on a journey towards embodying the happiest, healthiest, most whole version of myself for the greater part of 9 years now.

I've tried all of the supplements, all the "biohacks" and all of the self help advice. I've spent thousands of dollars on my own education and countless hours reading, studying and implementing.

And while these financial and time investments have completely changed my life for the better, I didn't feel complete just keeping all of the knowledge I have gained just for myself.

That is why I created Keep Your Chi Up.

To have a place where I can take all of the fruits that I have been able to bear from years of dedication to this process and share them with others who are also seeking to become the best version of themselves in this lifetime.

I have a number of options for people who are seeking to reap some of the same benefits in their own life that I have in mine — to get to a place where you feel like you're the driver of your own life and have the tools and frameworks to be your own healer.

Because no one is coming to save us.

It is up to every individual to take responsibility over the life we are creating moment to moment in order to embody the best version of ourselves and to live in alignment with what we want and need physically, mentally, emotionally and spiritually.
